Hosted Tutorials

When I got tired of explaining Facebook to my mom, I decided to create a browser extension that lets people create and share tutorials on any website. With my browser plugin, if my mom wanted to know how to post something on Facebook or send an email in Gmail, I could just click on the area of the web-app in question and fill out a simple form. The result is a popover with text or a YouTube video explaining everything I wanted her to know -- but in context and in front of her eyes.

The code is a mess (it was my first web project), but you can check out the source here. I ultimately want to rewrite it with a django back-end.
