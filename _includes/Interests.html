Interests

Travel

My wife, Lucia, and I love traveling together. A couple of years ago most of our travel was within the Americas and Europe. When I was offered the chance to work and live in Sydney, Australia, we jumped at the opportunity. How could we pass that up?

Since our move we've seen some of most naturally beautiful places in the world, and also some of the most historical. We've been truly fortunate.

Just before our move, we decided to keep an online diary of our trips so our families could keep up to date -- while we're a few trips behind, feel free to read about some of the places we've been to.

Technology and Startups

Coming from a strong technology background, I've found it hard to stay away 100% -- despite my job being much more business focused these days. I try to keep as up to date as I can on tech trends through reading and hands on experience.

Entrepreneurship has also been an interest of mine for a while. While in Boston, I participated in python and django hack nights, Startup Weekend, and other meetups that were geared towards the startup scene. I've kept up with this a bit in Sydney, but to a lesser extent.

While I don't write down my thoughts about entrepreneurship or technology much, it does happen occasionally and those thoughts can be found here.

Reading

Before coming to Sydney, I hadn't been a keen reader. As part of our "fresh start", I suggested my wife and I avoid getting a TV, and instead spend better quality time together. While this did turn into a bit of huddling around my 13" MacBook Pro to watch streamed episodes of homeland -- a lot of my time with Lucia turned into exploring the city, listening to interesting podcasts together, and, of course, reading.

I went from about 2 books a year to 13 and it was fantastic. A large part of the change was finding my love of novels (I used to only read non-fiction because I thought it was a waste of time otherwise...says the guy watching hours of TV) -- but I still find the time to read a business or management book here and there.
